# AI Maritime Risk Intelligence Platform

## Overview

AI Maritime Risk Intelligence Platform is a web-based logistics intelligence system designed to analyze maritime shipping routes, predict transportation risks, recommend alternative ports, and provide operational analytics for waterway logistics.

The platform combines machine learning, route analysis, seasonal forecasting, and interactive visualization to support maritime decision-making.

---

## Features

### Route Risk Analysis

* Analyze routes between global seaports
* Calculate route distance and estimated transit time
* Classify routes into Low, Medium, or High risk categories
* Generate risk explanations based on route conditions

### Alternative Route Optimization

* Recommend alternative destination ports for high-risk routes
* Compare original and optimized routes
* Evaluate route efficiency and operational impact

### Seasonal Risk Forecasting

* Month-based route analysis
* Seasonal weather risk assessment
* Congestion-aware route evaluation
* Dynamic risk variation based on time of year

### Interactive Mapping

* Global maritime route visualization
* Origin and destination port display
* Alternative route mapping
* Risk-aware route rendering

### Analytics Dashboard

* Route performance metrics
* Risk distribution analysis
* Cost intelligence reporting
* Seasonal trend analysis
* Historical route analytics

### Risk Prediction Engine

* Machine learning-based risk prediction
* Logistics parameter analysis
* Risk score generation
* Operational recommendations
